QR Decomposition

QR decomposition or QR factorization is a decomposition of a matrix $\Mat{A}$ into a product, \Mat{A} = \Mat{Q} \Mat{R} of an orthogonal matrix $\Mat{Q}$ and an upper triangular matrix $\Mat{R}$. If $\Mat{A}$ is square and invertible, then the factorization is unique if we require the diagonal elements of $\Mat{R}$ to be positive.
