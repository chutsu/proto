Forward Substitution

\Mat{A} \Vec{x} = \Vec{b} \begin{bmatrix} a_{11} & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & 0 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \dots & a_{mn} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \\ \vdots \\ x_{m} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} b_{1} \\ b_{2} \\ \vdots \\ b_{m} \end{bmatrix} writing out the above, &a_{11} x_{1} = b_{1} \\ &a_{21} x_{1} + a_{22} x_{2} = b_{2} \\ &a_{31} x_{1} + a_{32} x_{2} + a_{33} x_{3} = b_{3} \\ &\qquad\qquad\qquad\vdots \\ &a_{m,1} x_{1} + a_{m,2} x_{2} + \dots + a_{m,n} x_{n} = b_{n} and rearranging to solve for $\Vec{x}$, x_{1} &= b_{1} / a_{11} \\ x_{2} &= (b_{2} - a_{21} x_{1}) / a_{22} \\ x_{3} &= (b_{3} - a_{31} x_{1} - a_{32} x_{2} ) / a_{33} \\ &\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\vdots \\ x_{m} &= (b_{m} - a_{m,1} x_{1} - a_{m,2} x_{2} - \dots - a_{m,m-1} x_{m-1} ) / a_{m,n} or more generally, \boxed{ x_{i} = \dfrac{b_{i} - \sum_{j=1}^{i-1} a_{ij} x_{i}}{a_{ii}} \quad \text{where} \; 1 \leq i \leq n }.
